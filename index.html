About Ruze

Ruze Fashion was established in 2010 in Edmonton Alberta, Canada by two Accountants who noticed that traditional messenger bags were too boring, even by accountants standards. Also the weight of textbooks and laptops weren’t able to be supported by most messenger bags. This transformed into an idea to make the best eco-friendly fashion accessories for students and professionals to use around the world.

What we do

Ruze Fashion is the premier manufacturer of recycled fashion accessories. Ruze Fashion’s products are all made from recycled PVC advertisements, our products include:

  • Messenger bags;
  • Backpacks;
  • Gym bags;
  • Laptop sleeves;
  • Wallets and;
  • Re-useable grocery bags

How we do it

Ruze Fashion sets themselves apart from other companies in the recycled fashion industry by providing high quality and durable products at great value. We’re able to provide great quality products through the use of only the highest quality PVC (vinyl) advertisements in all of our products. All pieces of PVC used in Ruze Fashion’s products come from businesses who understand and appreciate the harmful effect that PVC has on the environment.

Corporate Partnership

If Your company uses PVC/Vinyl advertisements in campaigns we would like to form a partnership with your organization to help reduce the amount of Vinyl advertisements that end up in landfills around the world.

The partnership works by providing your companies used PVC to us in the production of our messenger bags, backpacks, gym bags, laptop sleeves, wallets or re-usable grocery bags. We will then provide the products back to your company in the form of promotional items or merchandise for employees.

Benefits to your business:

  1. Prolong the life of you advertising campaign
  2. Provide employees with unique and fashionable accessories
  3. Help to divert unnecessary waste in our landfills.
